Who We Are

A Home Built on Care & Community

Eden House Care Facility is a community-oriented nursing home and retirement home nestled in the countryside between Rockwood, Eden Mills and Guelph. Surrounded by lush grounds and a Memorial Garden, we have been a pillar in the community for over 50 years.

Together with our staff, professional associates, and dedicated volunteers, we strive to give residents the best possible care — making Eden House truly "a nice place to live."

Eden House believes in a people-centred approach to care. It is licensed by the Ministry of Long-Term Care and Retirement Homes Regulatory Authority, working in partnership with residents and their families.

What Guides Us

Mission, Vision & Values

Every decision at Eden House is guided by a clear set of principles that place residents and their families at the centre of everything we do.

Mission

Eden House is committed to providing exemplary long-term care services to Residents. Our focus is on the provision of individualized care that respects, supports and enables Residents to be as independent as possible.

Vision

Eden House strives to be a leader in the long-term care field, recognized for high quality care and service and its commitment to working in partnership with Residents and their families. We strive to design and deliver programming that meets the growing and changing needs of Residents and the community we serve, and to provide a rewarding and empowering work environment.

Our Core Values

Accountability

Individuals and groups are accountable for the tasks delegated to them.

Compassion

We respect individual feelings, needs and wishes in all care.

Excellence

We strive to provide the highest quality care and service at all times.

Teamwork

We believe we achieve more working together toward a common purpose.

Innovation

Creativity assists us in achieving effective and efficient use of resources.

Safety

We create a culture of safety for residents, visitors, volunteers and staff.

Two Paths to Peace of Mind

Long-Term Care Home

  • Licensed by the Ministry of Health and Long-Term Care
  • Resident-centred nursing & personal care 24 hours per day
  • Private, semi, and ward accommodations
  • Medical care and physician supervision on-site
  • Pharmacy services & daily medication assistance
  • Daily housekeeping and laundry services
  • Intimate setting — not a large institutional facility
  • Three meals per day with menu-style service & snacks
  • Dietician assessment and therapeutic diets & supplements
  • Assistance with all activities of daily living

Retirement Home

  • Private accommodation including hydro, heat and water
  • Emergency response system throughout the facility
  • 24-hour supervision by trained staff

  • Weekly bath preparation assistance

Health & Safety

Infection Prevention
& Control

The safety of our residents, staff, and visitors is our highest priority. Eden House has comprehensive infection prevention and control protocols aligned with Ontario public health guidelines and Ministry of Long-Term Care directives.

Personal Protective Equipment

Staff follow strict PPE protocols at all times. All required equipment is provided and properly donned, doffed, and disposed of per public health guidance.

🧼

Hand Hygiene Program

Rigorous hand hygiene policies are enforced throughout the facility for all staff, residents, and visitors — a cornerstone of our IPC program.

Active Screening Protocols

Regular screening of residents, staff and visitors with rapid response procedures and isolation protocols for any identified concerns.

Ministry Compliance
