Who We Are

A Community
Cornerstone Since 1968

Eden House is a people-centred care home surrounded by lush grounds and a beautiful Memorial Garden. Licensed by the Ministry of Long-Term Care and the Retirement Homes Regulatory Authority. Together with our staff, professional associates and volunteers, we strive every day to make Eden House "a nice place to live."

What Guides Us

Mission, Vision
& Values

Every decision at Eden House is guided by principles that place residents and their families at the centre of everything we do.

Mission

Eden House is committed to providing exemplary long-term care services to Residents. Our focus is on individualized care that respects, supports and enables Residents to be as independent as possible.

Vision

Eden House strives to be a leader in the long-term care field, recognized for high quality care and its commitment to working in partnership with Residents and their families. We strive to design and deliver programming that meets the growing and changing needs of Residents and the community we serve.

Our Core Values

Accountability

Individuals and groups are accountable for tasks delegated to them.

Compassion

We respect individual feelings, needs and wishes in all care.

Excellence

We strive to provide the highest quality care in everything we do.

Teamwork

We achieve more by working together for a common purpose.

Innovation

Creativity assists us in achieving effective use of resources.

Safety

We create a culture of safety for all who live and work here.

Customer Focus

We design our work processes with a focus on satisfaction.

Ethical Care

Decisions are sensitive to moral, cultural and personal issues.
