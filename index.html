About Eden House

A Community
Cornerstone Since 1968

Eden House is a people-centered care home surrounded by lush grounds and a beautiful Memorial Garden. Licensed by the Ministry of Long-Term Care and the Retirement Homes Regulatory Authority, our home is committed to providing exemplary care that respects, supports, and enables residents to be as independent as possible.

Together with our staff, professional associates and volunteers, we strive every day to make Eden House "a nice place to live."
