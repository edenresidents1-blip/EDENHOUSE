Features

Everything Your
Loved One Deserves

Eden House Care Facility is committed to providing exemplary services to our residents. Our focus is on individualized care that respects, supports and enables residents to be as independent as possible.

  • Licensed by the Ministry of Health and Long-Term Care
  • Resident-centred nursing and personal care 24 hours per day
  • Private, semi-private and ward accommodations available
  • Medical care and supervision available within the facility
  • On-site pharmacy services
  • Daily housekeeping, laundry and medication assistance
  • Intimate, home-like setting surrounded by lush grounds
  • Assistance with all activities of daily living
  • Three meals per day with menu-style service, plus morning, afternoon and evening snacks
  • Dietician assessment, therapeutic diets and dietary supplements

Day-to-Day Life

What to Expect at
Eden House

🍽️
Dining

Three nutritious meals daily with menu-style service, plus snacks throughout the day. Dietician assessments ensure therapeutic and supplemental needs are met.

🎭
Recreation

Our full-time recreation team delivers daily activities, outings, and evening entertainment — keeping residents engaged, social, and joyful.

💊
Medical Care

On-site pharmacy, medical supervision, and nursing care around the clock. Professional associates available to support specialized health needs.

🧹
Housekeeping

Daily housekeeping and personal laundry services ensure a clean, comfortable and dignified living environment for every resident.

🌿
Grounds & Garden

Beautiful lush grounds including a Memorial Garden — a peaceful retreat for residents, families and visitors to enjoy together.

👨‍👩‍👧
Family Partnership

We work in close partnership with residents' families, maintaining open communication and involving loved ones in care planning.
