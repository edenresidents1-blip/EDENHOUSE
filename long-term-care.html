Ministry Licensed · 24-Hour Care

Care That Feels
Like Home

Eden House Long-Term Care Home is a 58-bed facility licensed by the Ministry of Long-Term Care, providing 24-hour resident-centred nursing and personal care. We are not a large institutional facility — we are an intimate, home-like setting where staff know every resident by name.

Situated in a beautiful countryside setting between Rockwood, Eden Mills and Guelph, Eden House provides a peaceful, dignified environment where residents can live as independently as possible, with the full support they need.

What We Provide

Comprehensive
Care Services

Eden House is committed to providing exemplary care. Our focus is individualized service that respects, supports and enables every resident to be as independent as possible.

🩺

Nursing & Medical Care

24-hour resident-centred nursing and personal care provided by Registered Nurses and Registered Practical Nurses. On-site physician supervision and regular medical rounds.

💊

Pharmacy Services

On-site pharmacy services and daily medication assistance ensure residents receive their prescribed medications safely, correctly and on schedule every day.

🍽️

Nutrition & Dining

Three nutritious meals per day with a menu-style service, plus morning, afternoon and evening snacks. Registered dietitian assessment, therapeutic diets and supplement programs.

🛁

Personal Care

Assistance with all activities of daily living — bathing, dressing, grooming, toileting and mobility — provided with dignity, patience and genuine compassion by our PSW team.

🧹

Housekeeping & Laundry

Daily housekeeping keeps resident rooms and common areas clean and comfortable. Personal laundry and bed linens are laundered daily and returned to residents.

🎭

Recreation & Activities

Full-time recreation team delivering daily programming — daytime and evening. Music, arts & crafts, exercise, social events, community outings and seasonal celebrations.

🦷

Dental Services

On-site dental services are available for residents, ensuring oral health care is accessible and convenient without requiring transport to outside appointments.

👣

Foot Care

Regular foot care services are provided on-site, an important component of overall health and comfort especially for diabetic or mobility-impaired residents.

💇

Hair Salon

An on-site hair salon provides residents with regular hair care services — an important part of dignity, self-expression and overall wellbeing.

🕊️

Pastoral & Spiritual Care

Pastoral care services support residents' spiritual and emotional wellbeing, respecting and honouring each resident's individual beliefs and traditions.

🔒

Safe Wandering Area

A dedicated safe wandering area within the facility provides freedom of movement for residents with cognitive impairments in a secure and monitored environment.

🌿

Grounds & Memorial Garden

Beautiful landscaped grounds and a peaceful Memorial Garden give residents and families a serene outdoor space to enjoy the fresh countryside air.

Room Types

Accommodation Options

Eden House offers three types of accommodation to suit residents' needs and preferences. All rooms include the same high standard of care and services.

Private Room

Single occupancy · Maximum privacy
  • Your own private room — no roommates
  • Personal furniture and décor welcome
  • Full window with natural light
  • Private closet and storage space
  • All standard care services included
  • Choice of basic, preferred or enhanced

Semi-Private Room

Two-bed room · Shared with one other resident
  • Shared room with one other resident
  • Privacy curtain between beds
  • Personal storage and closet space
  • Opportunity for social connection
  • All standard care services included
  • Government co-payment rate may apply

Ward Room

Multi-bed room · Most economical option
  • Shared room with two or more residents
  • Privacy curtains between beds
  • Personal storage space provided
  • Vibrant social environment
  • All standard care services included
  • Lowest government co-payment rate

Room availability varies. Contact us to discuss current openings and placement options.

How to Apply

Admissions Process

Placement in a long-term care home in Ontario is coordinated through your local Home and Community Care Support Services (formerly LHIN). Here's what to expect.

1

Contact Home and Community Care

Begin by contacting your local Home and Community Care Support Services (HCCSS) to request an assessment for long-term care placement. They will evaluate eligibility and care needs.

2

Complete the Assessment

A care coordinator from HCCSS will conduct a thorough assessment to determine your level of care needs and eligibility for long-term care. This assessment determines the care you require.

3

Choose Eden House

Once approved, you can choose up to five long-term care homes. We encourage you to visit Eden House in person and meet our team before making your selection.

4

Wait for a Bed to Become Available

Your placement coordinator will contact you when a bed becomes available at Eden House. You have 24–48 hours to accept the offer of a bed.

5

Move In & Settle In

Our team will welcome you and your family on move-in day and help you settle into your new home. We conduct a detailed care conference within the first few weeks to review your individual care plan.
