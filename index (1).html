Who We Are

Community-rooted care since 1968

Nestled between Rockwood, Eden Mills, and Guelph, Eden House is a 58-bed long-term care home and 20-bed retirement home licensed by the Ministry of Long-Term Care and the Retirement Homes Regulatory Authority.

Our team of nurses, personal support workers, recreation staff, and volunteers work together each day to create an environment where residents feel at home — respected, engaged, and genuinely cared for.
