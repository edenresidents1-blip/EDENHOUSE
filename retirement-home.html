Retirement Home

20 private suites with meals, housekeeping, 24-hour supervision, and a full daily activities program — all in a warm, home-like setting.

Independent Living with Support

Your Own Home,
with Care Around You

The Eden House Retirement Home offers 20 private suites for seniors who value their independence but appreciate knowing support is always close by. Our retirement home is licensed by the Retirement Homes Regulatory Authority (RHRA) and provides a full range of services so residents can focus on living well.

What's Included

Everything Taken Care Of

Our retirement home suite package covers the essentials so residents can live with dignity and focus on the things they enjoy.

🏠

Private Accommodation

Each suite is fully private and includes hydro, heat and water. Bedroom furniture is provided including a bedside table and dresser. Residents are welcome to personalize their space.

🍽️

Three Meals Daily

Nutritious, menu-style dining three times a day plus morning, afternoon and evening snacks. Meals are prepared fresh and served in a social dining environment.

🔔

Emergency Response System

A 24-hour emergency call system throughout the building ensures help is always immediately available. Staff are on-site around the clock for peace of mind.

👁️

24-Hour Supervision

Trained staff are on duty 24 hours a day, 7 days a week — providing supervision, assistance, and a familiar, caring presence for every resident.

💊

Medication Administration

Medication administration and supervision ensures residents take their prescribed medications safely, correctly, and on schedule — every day.

🛁

Bath Preparation Assistance

Weekly bath preparation assistance is provided by trained care staff, supporting residents with bathing safely and comfortably.

🧹

Housekeeping Services

Regular housekeeping ensures suites and common areas are always clean and comfortable. A tidy, well-maintained home environment is a top priority.

👕

Daily Laundry Service

Personal clothing and bed linens are laundered daily, returned clean and folded. Residents never need to worry about their laundry.

🎭

Daily Activities Programming

Our full-time recreation team runs daily programs tailored to residents' interests and abilities — from crafts and music to exercise, outings, and special events.

🚌

Community Outings

Organized outings into the community give residents opportunities to stay connected with the wider world, explore local attractions, and enjoy social experiences.
