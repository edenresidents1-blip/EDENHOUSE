Our Story

Rooted in community

Eden House Care Facility is a community-oriented 58-bed nursing home and 20-bed retirement home nestled between Rockwood, Eden Mills and Guelph, Ontario.

The nursing and retirement home has been a pillar in the community for over 55 years, excelling and distinguishing itself in resident care. It is licensed by the Ministry of Long-Term Care and the Retirement Homes Regulatory Authority.

Together with our staff, professional associates, and our volunteers, we continue to strive to give residents the best possible care — making Eden House "a nice place to live."

Our Mission

Individualized care that respects and enables

Eden House Care Facility is committed to providing exemplary services to our residents. Our focus is on the provision of individualized care that respects, supports and enables residents to be as independent as possible.

We are proud members of the Registered Nurses Association of Ontario and uphold the highest standards of professional care and ethical practice.
