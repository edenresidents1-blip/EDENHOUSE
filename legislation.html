Resident Rights

Your rights as a resident

All residents of Eden House are entitled to a full set of rights protected under provincial legislation. These include the right to:

Be treated with courtesy, respect, and dignity
Receive care and assistance in accordance with your assessed needs
Be informed of your care plan and participate in decisions about your care
Have visitors at reasonable times
Raise concerns and have complaints addressed promptly
Access your own health records
Live in a safe environment free from abuse and neglect

For a full copy of your rights, please speak with our administration team or refer to the relevant provincial legislation above.
